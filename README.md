🍔 Foodpanda Sales & Operational Performance Analysis
📊 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive sales and operational analysis of Foodpanda’s transactional dataset. The objective was to evaluate revenue performance, customer behavior, payment preferences, geographic contribution, and restaurant/category performance.

The dashboard and analysis were built to uncover actionable business insights that can support strategic decision-making.

🎯 Business Objectives
Evaluate total revenue performance
Identify top and low-performing locations
Analyze monthly sales trends
Assess payment method preferences
Compare restaurant performance
Analyze product category contribution
📈 Key Performance Metrics
Metric	Value
Total Sales	$4,803,149
Total Orders	6,000
Total Quantity Sold	17,950
🌍 Sales by Location
Multan recorded the highest sales: $1,009,003.67
Karachi recorded the lowest sales: $900,176.82

🔎 Insight:
While all cities performed strongly, Multan demonstrated superior revenue contribution, suggesting higher demand concentration or stronger customer retention strategies in that region.

📅 Monthly Sales Performance
September recorded the highest monthly sales: $441,252.45

🔎 Insight:
This indicates potential seasonal demand spikes, promotional effectiveness, or festive-driven sales growth during that period.

💳 Payment Method Analysis
Payment Method	Sales
Cash	$1.65M
Card	$1.59M
Wallet	$1.56M

🔎 Insight:
Cash remains the most preferred payment method, generating the highest revenue contribution. However, digital payments (Card + Wallet) are nearly equivalent, showing strong adoption of cashless transactions.

🍽 Restaurant Performance

🍝 Category Performance
Category	Sales
Italian	$1,005,523.03

🔎 Insight:
Italian cuisine dominated category sales, while Dessert recorded the lowest performance. Despite this, all categories maintained strong revenue contribution.

📊 Key Business Insights
Revenue distribution across cities is relatively balanced, reducing geographic risk.
Cash still dominates, but digital payments are closing the gap.
Brand competition is strong with no extreme outliers.
Category performance shows clear customer preference patterns.
September demand spike suggests seasonal opportunity optimization.

📌 Conclusion

The analysis shows stable revenue distribution, strong brand competition, and diversified payment behavior. Strategic focus areas include:

Strengthening digital payment incentives
Expanding high-performing categories
Leveraging seasonal sales peaks
Boosting lower-performing cities
